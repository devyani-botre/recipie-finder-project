Ingredients: 200g pasta (spaghetti or penne), 2 tbsp olive oil, 3 garlic cloves (minced), 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved), fresh basil leaves, salt, pepper, and grated Parmesan cheese.

Procedure:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add cherry tomatoes and cook until softened.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta into the pan and mix well.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh basil leaves.
  6. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and serve hot.

15-Minute Pasta
Quick Stir-Fry

Ingredients: 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, broccoli, bell peppers), 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp oyster sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 garlic clove (minced), and 1 tbsp cornstarch (dissolved in water).

Procedure:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan and sauté garlic until golden.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes.
  3. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch mixture.
  4. Stir until the sauce thickens and coats the vegetables evenly.
  5. Serve with steamed rice or noodles.

Ingredients: 6 small tortillas, 1 cup cooked ground beef or chicken, 1 cup shredded lettuce, 1/2 cup diced tomatoes, 1/2 cup shredded cheese, salsa, and sour cream.

Procedure:

  1. Heat tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  2. Fill each tortilla with ground beef or chicken.
  3. Add lettuce, tomatoes, and shredded cheese.
  4. Top with salsa and sour cream.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
