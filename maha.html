Modak

Ingredients: Rice flour, jaggery, grated coconut, cardamom powder, ghee, water.

Procedure:

  1. Prepare dough using rice flour, water, and ghee.
  2. Make stuffing by cooking grated coconut with jaggery and cardamom powder.
  3. Shape dough into small cups, fill with stuffing, and seal to form modaks.
  4. Steam modaks in a steamer for 10-12 minutes.

Vada Pav

Ingredients: Potatoes, garlic, green chilies, mustard seeds, turmeric, gram flour, pav, oil, salt.

Procedure:

  1. Boil and mash potatoes. Add garlic, green chilies, mustard seeds, turmeric, and salt.
  2. Form potato mixture into balls, dip in gram flour batter, and deep-fry until golden brown.
  3. Serve hot vada in pav with chutneys.

Ingredients: Chicken/mutton, yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, lemon juice, salt, saffron milk.

Procedure:

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt, spices, and herbs for at least 2-3 hours.
  2. Cook basmati rice with whole spices and salt until 70% done.
  3. Layer the marinated meat and rice in a pot, add saffron milk, fried onions, and herbs.
  4. Seal the pot and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes.
  5. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad.
