Ingredients: Spaghetti, olive oil, garlic, tomatoes, fresh basil, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.

Procedure:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until golden.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until softened.
  4. Mix in the cooked spaghetti and season with salt, pepper, and basil.
  5. Garnish with Parmesan cheese and serve hot.

Indian Curry

Ingredients: Chicken, onions, garlic, ginger, tomatoes, curry powder, coconut milk, and oil.

Procedure:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until golden.
  2. Add chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add curry powder, chopped tomatoes, and coconut milk. Simmer until chicken is fully cooked.
  4. Serve hot with rice or naan.

Ingredients: Sushi rice, nori sheets, fresh fish (salmon/tuna), cucumber, avocado, and soy sauce.

Procedure:

  1. Cook sushi rice and season with rice vinegar.
  2. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice evenly over it.
  3. Add thin slices of fish, cucumber, and avocado on top.
  4. Roll tightly using the bamboo mat and slice into pieces.
  5. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
